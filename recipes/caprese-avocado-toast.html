Caprese Avocado Toast

Start to love cherry tomatoes and mozzarella in this healthy and simple avocado toast that’s perfect for a favorite breakfast, lunch and snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of toasted bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 4 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp fresh mozzarella
  • balsamic glaze

Steps

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown and mash the avocado evenly on top.
  2. Layer the sliced cherry tomatoes and diced mozzarella over the avocado.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and enjoy immediately.
