Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Elevate your ramen noodles with melted cheese to create a creamy, comfort-food meal in minutes. You can customize this recipe with eggs, veggies, or extra cheese for fresh flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring water to a boil in saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 mins
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
